A Letter For You

Dear Shikhar,

I don't really know where to begin, because there are so many things I wish I could put into words.

From the day we started talking, something quietly changed in me. I started looking forward to every notification, every conversation, every little interaction we shared.

Somehow, without even realizing it, you became a part of my daily thoughts.

I love the way you smile.
I love listening to you talk and hearing your thoughts.
I love the way you express yourself, even in the smallest moments.

What makes you special to me isn't just one thing. It's everything about you—the way you think, the way you care, the way you carry yourself, and the strength you have even when life gets difficult.

You may not always realize it, but there is something incredibly rare about you. Your presence feels comforting. Your words stay in my mind longer than you probably know.

I admire how strong you are. Even after everything life puts in front of you, you keep moving forward. That strength, that resilience… it’s beautiful.

I know you have your own struggles, your own past, your own battles. I'm not asking you to forget any of it. I'm not asking you to force feelings either.

I simply wanted you to know how deeply special you are to me.

Whether we're talking for hours or just saying goodnight, those little moments mean more to me than I can explain.

You inspire me to become a better person. You make me want to grow, understand more, care more, and love more gently.

I genuinely want to see you happy. I want to see you successful, peaceful, and surrounded by everything you deserve.

If one day I get the chance to make you smile a little more than yesterday, I'd consider myself truly lucky.

Thank you for simply being you.
